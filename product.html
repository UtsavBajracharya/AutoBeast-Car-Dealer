Sports Cars

Sports cars are high-performance, sleek vehicles designed for speed and agility. Characterized by low profiles, aerodynamic designs, and powerful engines, they prioritize driving dynamics and exhilarating experiences.

Monster Trucks

Monster trucks are oversized vehicles with enormous wheels, designed for off-road events, showcasing powerful stunts, jumps, and crushing obstacles.

Two Wheelers

Two-wheelers are motorized vehicles with two wheels, such as motorcycles and scooters, offering efficient, nimble, and often affordable transportation.

Mini Vans

Mini vans are compact, versatile vehicles with sliding doors, accommodating 7-8 passengers. Ideal for families, they prioritize space and convenience.

Electric Cars

Electric cars run on electricity, emitting zero tailpipe emissions. They use batteries for power, reducing environmental impact and dependence on fossil fuels.

Gears & Tools

Gears are mechanical components that transmit motion, while tools are instruments for tasks. Both crucial for various applications and industries.
